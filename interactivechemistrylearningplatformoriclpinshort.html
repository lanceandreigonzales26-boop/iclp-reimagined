About This Project

Built by students,
for students.

The Interactive Chemistry Learning Platform is a student-led initiative developed for Advanced Chemistry under Ms. Lorraine A. Voloso. It combines topic summaries, interactive browser-based mini-games, and Tinkercad 3D molecular models to turn passive review into active exploration.

The platform covers all four quarters of the curriculum and is designed to remain a useful study resource long after the school year concludes.
